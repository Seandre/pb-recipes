Rustic Bread

This recipe comes together in a bit less than 4 hours with poolish ready

Ingredients

Poolish

  • 150g Room Temp Water
  • 1/8th Tsp. Yeast
  • 150g AP Flour

Dough

  • POOLISH
  • 280g Water @ 100F
  • 2g Yeast
  • 10g Kosher Salt
  • 50g Whole Wheat Flour
  • 350g All Purpose Flour

Instructions

  1. Mix Poolish with water and yeast, then add whole wheat and all purpose flour, and salt
  2. Rest 30 minutes
  3. Stretch/tuck and fold
  4. Rest 30 minutes
  5. Stretch/tuck and fold
  6. Rest 1 hour
  7. Flour surface and dough liberally, flipping dough onto surface
  8. Pinch and fold opposite sides into center, then pinch together opposite sites
  9. Rorate and add tension to dough
  10. Flip seam side up into floured proofing basket
  11. Preheat dutch oven to 500F and Proof about 45 mins
  12. Bake at 485F for 18 minutes, covered
  13. Remove cover and bake an additional 25-30 minutes
